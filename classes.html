Yoga Classes


Gentle Hatha Yoga

Intended for beginners and anyone wishing a grounded foundation in the practice of yoga, this 60 minute of poses
and slow movement focuses on asana (proper alignment and posture), pranayama (breath work), and guided meditiation
to foster your mind and body connection.

Vinyasa Yoga

Although designed for intermediate to advanced students, begginers are welcome to sample this 60 minute class that
focuses on breath-synchronized movement-you will inhale and exhale as you flow energetically though yoga poses.

Restorative Yoga

This 90 minute class features very slow movement and long poses athat are supported by a chair or wall. This calming,
resorative experience is suitable for students of any level of experience. This practice can be a perfect way to help
re habilitate an injury.

Relax AnyTime with Savasana

Prepare yourself for savasana. Lie down on your yoga mat woth your arms at your side with palms
up. Close your eyes and breath slowly but deeply. Sink into the mat and let your worries slip away.
When you are ready, roll on your side and use your arms to push yourself to a sitting position with
crossed legs. Play your hands in a prayer position. Be grateful for all the things that you have in life.
Namaste.
